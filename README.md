# Gnanamani Engineering Industries — Official Website

A fully responsive, animated business website built for **Gnanamani Engineering Industries Pvt. Ltd.**, a metal fabrication company based in Cuddalore, Tamil Nadu, specializing in storage tanks, pressure vessels, reactors, and industrial process equipment.

## About the Project

Designed and developed end-to-end over **15 days**, this project covered the full lifecycle of building a real client website — from initial wireframing and brand interpretation, through cinematic UI design, to live deployment and working enquiry-form integration.

The goal was to give a traditional, decades-old engineering manufacturing business a modern digital presence that reflects the precision and craftsmanship of their work — without losing the trust and credibility expected in heavy industrial B2B services.

## What Was Built

- **Cinematic single-page design** — animated hero section, scroll-reveal sections, a custom spinning "forge" visual representing the company's metal fabrication identity
- **Fully responsive layout** — works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Live enquiry form** — integrated with Formspree, routing real customer enquiries directly to the business email
- **Direct contact actions** — one-tap Call and Email buttons for mobile visitors
- **Product showcase** — Storage Tanks, Pressure Vessels, Filter Press, Reactors, Heat Exchangers, Receivers
- **Specializations section** — SS/MS pipeline fabrication, structural fabrication, plant maintenance, pharmaceutical and chemical process equipment

## Tech Stack

- HTML5, CSS3 (custom animations, responsive grid/flexbox, no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript (scroll reveal via Intersection Observer, form handling)
- Formspree (form backend / email delivery)
- Deployed on Netlify

## Process

1. Gathered brand assets (company logo, signage, business registration details) directly from the client
2. Designed a wireframe-based structure reflecting the client's requested sections
3. Iterated through two design directions — a clean corporate layout and a bold cinematic/motion-led layout — landing on the cinematic version for stronger brand recall
4. Built a working contact form with real email delivery, tested end-to-end
5. Optimized for mobile responsiveness across breakpoints
6. Deployed live and connected for ongoing client use

## How to Run Locally
